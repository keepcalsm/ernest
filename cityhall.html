The Pasig City Hall

Constructed in the early 1960's with its original 4 story structure. Which is then renovated in the year 1999 making its floor area to approximately 9,000 square meters and in the year 2005 another 4 floors was added making it an eight (8) storey building. Under the current leadership of elected city mayor Vico Sotto, the once known Pasig City Hall will receive a new look and better improvements. Costing P9.6 billion on estimation according to some articles, the residents and media are awaiting on the changes and improvements that would be changed.
