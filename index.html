PASIG CITY , located in eastern Metro Manila, Philippines, spans approximately 31.5 square kilometers and has a population of over 800,000. Established in 1572 and officially becoming a city in 1994, it has significant historical importance, particularly during the Spanish colonial period. Today, Pasig serves as a major economic hub, especially in the Ortigas Center, with thriving industries like business process outsourcing (BPO) and retail. The city features the Pasig River and recreational spaces such as Pasig Rainforest Park, along with cultural events like the "Pasig City Fiesta." It is home to notable educational institutions, including Rizal High School, and is well-connected by public transport, making it a vibrant urban center.
